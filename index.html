About Me

I'm a Senior Software Development Engineer at Amazon with over 7 years of experience building scalable cloud solutions. Currently working on the CloudWatch team, I specialize in developing monitoring and visualization tools that help customers effectively manage their AWS resources.

I've since evolved into a passionate software engineer who loves developing innovative solutions, from Smart Garden systems that connect with Alexa to enterprise-scale cloud monitoring platforms.

As a Bar Raiser at Amazon, I contribute to maintaining high hiring standards and mentor candidates throughout the interview process. I enjoy coding, hiking, and exploring AI technologies in my spare time.
